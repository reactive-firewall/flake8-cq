SIM906: Experimental Rule to simplify code.

This rule will be renamed to SIM126 after its 6-month trial period is over. Please report any
issues you encounter with this rule to https://github.com/MartinThoma/flake8-simplify

The trial period starts on 20th of February and will end on 20th of September 2022.

Complex Form:

os.path.join(a, os.path.join(b, c))


Simplified From:

os.path.join(a, b, c)
