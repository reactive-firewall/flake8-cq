SIM118: explicit call to 'keys()' is already implied.

Config Options:
You might have good reasons to ignore some flake8 rules. To do that, use the standard Flake8
configuration. For example:

[flake8]
extend-ignore = SIM118


Complex form:

key in a_dict.keys()


Simplified form:

key in a_dict
