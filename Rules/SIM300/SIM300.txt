SIM300: Use 'age == 42' instead of '42 == age'.

It's way more common to have the variable first.

Complex Form:

"Yoda" == i_am


Simplified From:

i_am == "Yoda"


Rationale:
Both notations are equivalent in Python 3, but the second one is more pythonic.
