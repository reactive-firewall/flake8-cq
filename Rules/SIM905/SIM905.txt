SIM905: Experimental Rule to simplify code.

This rule will be renamed to SIM225 after its 6-month trial period is over. Please report any
issues you encounter with this rule to https://github.com/MartinThoma/flake8-simplify

The trial period starts on 13th of February and will end on 13th of September 2022.

Complex Form:

domains = "de com net org".split()


Simplified From:

domains = ["de", "com", "net", "org"]
