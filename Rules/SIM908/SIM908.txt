SIM908: Experimental Rule to simplify code.

This rule will be renamed to SIM121 after its 6-month trial period is over. Please report any
issues you encounter with this rule to https://github.com/MartinThoma/flake8-simplify

The trial period starts on 28th of March and will end on 28th of September 2022.

Complex Form:

name = "some_default"
if "some_key" in some_dict:
    name = some_dict["some_key"]


Simplified From:

name = some_dict.get("some_key", "some_default")
