SIM221: Use 'True' instead of 'a or not a'.

Simplify the tautology statement; Use 'True'.

Complex Form:

a or not a


Simplified From:

True


Rationale:
Both notations are semantically equivalent in Python 3, but the second one is shorter and faster.
