SIM904: Experimental Rule to simplify code.

This rule will be renamed to SIM224 after its 6-month trial period is over. Please report
any issues you encounter with this rule to https://github.com/MartinThoma/flake8-simplify

The trial period starts on 12th of February and will end on 12th of September 2022.

Complex Form:

a = {}
a["b"] = "c"


Simplified From:

a = {"b": "c"}
