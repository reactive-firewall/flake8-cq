SIM907: Experimental Rule to simplify code.

This rule will be renamed to SIM127 after its 6-month trial period is over. Please report
any issues you encounter with this rule to https://github.com/MartinThoma/flake8-simplify

The trial period starts on 28th of March and will end on 28th of September 2022.

Complex Form:

def foo(a: Union[int, None]) -> Union[int, None]:
    return a


Simplified From:

def foo(a: Optional[int]) -> Optional[int]:
    return a
