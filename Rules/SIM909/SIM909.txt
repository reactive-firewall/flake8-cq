SIM909: Experimental Rule to simplify code.

Thank you Ryan Delaney for the idea!

This rule will be renamed to SIM124 after its 6-month trial period is over. Please report any
issues you encounter with this rule to https://github.com/MartinThoma/flake8-simplify

The trial period starts on 28th of March and will end on 28th of September 2022.

Complex Form:

foo = foo


Simplified From:

# Nothing. Reflexive assignments have no purpose.
